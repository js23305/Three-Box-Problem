Three Box Probelm

Click on a door to make your choice:

Mathematical Explanation

There are three doors. Behind one door is a car; behind the other two doors, goats. You pick a door, then the host, who knows what's behind the doors, opens one of the other two doors, revealing a goat. You are then given the option: stick with your original pick or swich to the upopened door. What should you do?

  • You choose one of three doors(1/3 chance of picking the car).
  • The host, knowing where the car is, opens a door with a goat
  • You are given the choice to switch or stay

Should you switch?

Yes! If you stay, your chances of winning are1/3. If you switch your chances increase to2/3!
